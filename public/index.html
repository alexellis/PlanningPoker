Welcome to Planning Poke

This tool lets teams collaborate and estimate story points for SCRUM user stories without revealing what everyone is thinking until all the estimates are in. It has proven useful with distributed teams.

Instructions

  • Select 'Host a game'
  • Tell everyone the URL for the poker session
  • Wait for everyone you are expecting to join then type in the first story and click 'Play'
  • This sends the story to everyone and gives them a chance to estimate in private
  • As the host you will see a list of estimates coming in, when you have them all click 'Show Cards'.
  • Everyone's guesses will now be shared for discussion.
Enjoy.
